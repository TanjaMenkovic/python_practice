0. FizzBuzz in Python

The rules are that candidates should iterate over a predefined list of numbers, and:

Replace all integers that are evenly divisible by 3 with “fizz”
Replace all integers divisible by 5 with “buzz”
Replace all integers divisible by both 3 and 5 with “fizzbuzz”
Avoid using range() in their answer (This is optional but adds an extra step of complexity)
